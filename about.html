Flavor Savor

Find places based on what flavors they serve not just based on professionally written reviews.

We are currently available for Urbana-Champaign, Pittsburg and Las Vegas. We use the yelp dataset from the yelp data challenge.

Try out for Urbana-Champaign. Source code available at https://github.com/napsternxg/FlavorSavor

Problems we solve

Find flavors around

Majority of times people want to try out a given flavor on a given day depending on their mood. Finding this using the current restaurant review sites is tough. We solve this problem by using data mining of reviews posted by users to calculate the popular flavors of that restaurant across the 5 flavor categories specified in Cook Smart.

Write useful reviews

Most people don't go to restaurants to write reviews and most of the good reviews are written by professionals and hobbyist. The other reviews are not well structured and are hard to mine for useful information. We use the popular social media format of #hashtags to tag the various review options for the restaurant and then allow users to select from those options and post to their social stream. Due to the simple text nature of our review and because of the structure using #hashtags we are able to provide easy to use interface for users who just like to EAT and for companies who like to find out how and what their users eat.
